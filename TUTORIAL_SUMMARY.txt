📐 THE FORMULA (Quick Reference)

Battery Charge (%) = (Byte₀ + Byte₁ × 256) × 0.0025

Example: Bytes 40 9C
  = (0x40 + 0x9C × 256) × 0.0025
  = (64 + 156 × 256) × 0.0025
  = (64 + 39,936) × 0.0025
  = 40,000 × 0.0025
  = 100%

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

💡 KEY LEARNING POINTS

1. Little-Endian Byte Order
   Data: 40 9C → Read as: 9C 40
   (First byte is least significant)

2. Hexadecimal to Decimal
   9C40₁₆ = 40,000₁₀
   (Use calculator or online converter)

3. Apply Resolution
   40,000 × 0.0025 = 100%
   (Each bit = 0.0025%)

4. Special Values
   FF FF = Not Available
   (Vehicle off or sensor error)
